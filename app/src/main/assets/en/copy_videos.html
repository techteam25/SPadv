Copy and Share Video Files

Purpose: The purpose of the Copy / Share Videos feature is to copy or backup your videos to another location. This could be either on your phone, an SD card, an OnTheGo (OTG) flash drive or to a computer, so that if something happens to your device, you will still have a copy of all the videos you have published.

How to do it : To copy or backup your videos, select the videos from the list and tap the COPY button. You will need to pick a folder where you want the video files to be copied. You may select a folder from the internal phone memory, from the SD card in the phone, or from a device that you are connected to such as a computer or an OnTheGo flash drive.

It is a good idea to backup the Videos Folder regularly (i.e. once every two or three months) when videos are regularly being created just in case your device or SD card fails.

You can also share individual files that you select by tapping the share icon and then selecting how you want to share it.
